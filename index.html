North-East / East / North

The Entrance & Pooja

Width: Ideal 3.5ft - 4ft. Largest door in house (Simhadwara).
Design: Avoid cracked wood; use Teak or Sheesham.
Pooja Room: In North East to bring positivity.
Shoe Racks: Place in West or North-West; never in the North-East.
South-West

Master Suite

Bed Placement: South-West corner; headboard against South or West wall.
Sleeping Side: Head must face South (Longevity) or East (Memory).
Heavy Furniture: Wardrobes & Lockers against South/West walls.
Mirrors: Never reflect the bed; place on North/East walls.
South-East

Culinary & Dining

Stove (Fire): South-East. Cook faces East.
Sink (Water): North-East or North. Maintain gap from fire.
Dining: West or North-West. Wood tables only.
North / East

Study & Office Cabins

Direction: Desk faces North or East for concentration.
Lighting: Natural light from the left.
Games Room: Light game acitivities for kids mainly in North East.
Decor: Indoor fountain in the North-East for mental clarity.
North-West / North

Pool, Parking & Lawn

Swimming Pool: North or East quadrant for "Water" flow.
Car Parking: North-West (Movement) or South-East (Heat/Metal).
Stairs: South-West or West. Always climb clockwise.
North-West / South-East

Bath & Restrooms & Gyms

Commode Face: Facing North or South.
Ventilation: North or West windows.
Gyms and more:Heavier Games usually in South or South East.

Color Psychology & Interior Mending

Using Vastu colors isn't just tradition; it’s about the emotional resonance of space.

Cream/Off-White

Peace & Harmony. Best for Master Bedrooms to induce sleep.

Sky Blue

Trust & Calm. Ideal for North-facing living rooms.

Pale Yellow

Intellect & Energy. Perfect for Study rooms and Kitchens.

Soft Green

Growth & Healing. Best for Kids' rooms in the North-West.

Light Pink

Compassion. Excellent for Guest rooms to foster warmth.

Chocolate/Wood

Grounding. Use for heavy furniture in the South-West.

Subtle Improvement Tips:

  • Artworks: Avoid paintings of war or sadness. Use nature or spiritual motifs.
  • Indoor Fountains: Place in North-East. Ensure the water flows *toward* the center of the house.
  • Heavy Furniture: TVs and large cabinets must be on South or West walls to maintain the home's "Gravity Center".
  • Centre (Brahmasthan):Open space/ living area/ walkin passage/ calm spot for meditation; kept clean and clutter‑free.
